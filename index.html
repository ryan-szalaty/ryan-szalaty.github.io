April 3rd, 2023, 9:30AM. Golden-orange sunlight lit the brand-new space that I had moved into, and the freshly-brewed cup of dark-roast coffee wafted through the room. It was my second month of onboarding with a fintech company in Tokyo as an external recruiter. After spending eight months interviewing and finally being accepted into a great team, the first sips of morning coffee were sweeter than they had ever been. I was scheduled to have a morning chat with my manager, so I opened the Zoom link and was greeted by the usual solemn, yet friendly face.

His eyes, though, betrayed an intention to match the air of that lovely morning, and the next few words that quivered out of his mouth will forever remain in my memory.

"We are terminating your position due to redundancy."

April 3rd, 9:32AM. That moment flipped the 3-month hourglass that remained to find another job or face deportation. Although that time was my most humbling, it lit two different sparks, one of which would grow into a more potent flame.

The latter led me to produce the text you are kindly reading. Born out of necessity and built from a desire to carve out a better life, my desire to break into tech came to full light.
